Exercise 18

We now work on visualising our data.

p1 <- ggplot(data = team_data, 
             mapping = aes(x = github_username, 
                           y = favourite_number,
                           fill = colour), 
             ) +
   geom_col() +
   scale_fill_identity()
p1

I see a bar graph. The x-axis is labelled “github_username,” and has six values with each individual’s username. Each bar is the person’s favorite color. The y-axis is labelled “favourite_number,” and each bar reaches to the y-value of the person’s favourite number.
